EXPERIENCE ADVENTURE

Cozy Cabins

Bear Ridge offers two guests a cozy, fully furnished cabin for a romantic getaway in upper Buffalo River country. The cabin has a main floor and sleeping loft with a queen-size bed. We are pleased to offer complimentary WIFI with this cabin rental. The cabin's kitchen features a dining bar and is fully furnished with appliances, basic meal prep utensils and cookware, a coffee maker and even a dishwasher. In the cabin's bathroom, you'll find a lovely stone walk-in shower, along with towels, soap, and shampoo.

Courageous Canoes

In the mood for taking on an adventure through the river. Then come on and rent a canoe to embark on your journey through the beautiful Buffalo river. Our outfitter uses Buffalo Canoes, which are manufactured right here in Newton County Arkansas (https://buffalocanoemanufacturing.com/)
