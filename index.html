Why choose us

At Buffalo Retreat, our family-owned business ensures personalized service along the famous Buffalo National River. We go the extra mile to guarantee your comfort and enjoyment.

We provide top-notch canoes and gear, ensuring a safe and enjoyable river experience. Whether you’re a seasoned paddler or a first-timer, our equipment won’t disappoint.
